Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you service?

I provide barista services for a wide range of events including weddings, corporate parties, birthday celebrations, anniversary parties, coffee receptions and private dinners. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a large-scale event, I can customize my services to meet your needs.

Do you provide all the coffee equipment and supplies?

I bring all essential barista equipment including professional espresso machines, grinders, tools, cups and basic ingredients. For the coffee beans and specialty ingredients, we can either provide them for an additional fee or you can supply your own preferred brands. We'll discuss all these details during our consultation.

How far in advance should I book your services?

For weddings and large events, I recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak seasons (May-October). For smaller events, 1-2 months notice is usually sufficient, but I've accommodated last-minute requests when my schedule allows.

Can you create custom coffee drinks for my event?

Absolutely! Custom coffee drinks are one of my specialties. I'll work with you to develop signature drinks that reflect your event's theme, your personal tastes, or even incorporate specific ingredients that are meaningful to you. This service is included in my premium packages.

What's your cancellation policy?

I require a 50% deposit to secure your date, which is refundable up to 30 days before your event. For cancellations within 30 days, the deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to a future booking within one year. Full payment is due 14 days before the event.
